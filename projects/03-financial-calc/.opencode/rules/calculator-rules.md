# Financial Calculator Rules

## Compound Interest
- Formula: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt) + PMT * ((1 + r/n)^(nt) - 1) / (r/n)
- Show: final balance, total interest, yearly breakdown table, growth chart
- Inputs: principal, annual rate (1-20%), years (1-30), monthly contribution

## Depreciation
- Formula: Annual Depreciation = (Cost - Salvage) / Life
- Show: annual depreciation, yearly book value table, decline chart
- Inputs: asset cost, salvage value, useful life (1-50 years)

## ROI
- Formula: ROI% = ((Final - Initial) / Initial) * 100
- Show: ROI%, annualized ROI, comparison chart vs 10% benchmark
- Inputs: initial investment, final value, holding period years

## Loan Amortization
- Formula: M = P * (r(1+r)^n) / ((1+r)^n - 1)
- Show: monthly payment, total interest, full amortization table, pie chart
- Inputs: loan amount, annual rate, term years
